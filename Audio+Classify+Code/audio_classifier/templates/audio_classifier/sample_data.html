About UrbanSound8K Dataset

The UrbanSound8K dataset contains 8,732 labeled sound excerpts (≤4s) of urban sounds from 10 classes: air conditioner, car horn, children playing, dog bark, drilling, engine idling, gun shot, jackhammer, siren, and street music.

  • 8,732 audio files
  • 10 audio classes
  • 4-second maximum duration
  • Multiple formats
  • High quality audio
  • Well-labeled data
